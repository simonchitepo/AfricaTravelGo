Connectivity strategy

For smooth travel:

  • Activate your SIM/eSIM before leaving the airport when possible.
  • Download offline maps for key cities and park areas.
  • Keep important confirmations saved offline.

FAQ

Do I need a universal adapter?

Yes—plug types vary by country. A universal adapter covers most scenarios.

Is eSIM available?

In many destinations yes, but coverage varies. A local SIM is sometimes cheaper and more reliable.

How do I protect devices?

Use a simple dry bag, avoid exposing gear to dust, and keep backups of documents.

Should I bring a power strip?

Yes—one plug can charge multiple devices in older rooms with limited outlets.
