What we commit to

  • Clear, structured guides written for trip planning.
  • Visible update dates and correction paths.
  • Distinguishing fact from opinion and practical heuristics.
  • Linking to authoritative sources where helpful.

FAQ

How do you choose sources?

We prioritize official tourism boards, park authorities, health agencies, and on-the-ground operator policies.

How often do you update pages?

We update key planning pages when rules, seasons, or prices materially change and review major hubs regularly.

Can I request a correction?

Yes—use the Contact page and include the URL, the specific claim, and a supporting source.

Do you use affiliate links?

Some pages may include affiliate disclosures, but our planning content is written to be useful even without links.
