Documents checklist

Before you go:

  • Passport validity (6+ months recommended)
  • Visa/eVisa approval if needed
  • Vehicle papers if self-driving
  • Proof of onward travel if required
  • Cash for official fees

FAQ

Are border fees always official?

Not always. Ask for receipts and keep interactions polite but firm.

How long do crossings take?

It varies—from 30 minutes to several hours depending on traffic and systems.

Do I need passport photos?

Sometimes. Carry a few and keep photocopies of key documents.

What’s the biggest mistake?

Arriving late in the day and getting stuck after dark.
