How we choose destinations

We prioritize places that consistently deliver value to travelers: strong “first-time” experiences, clear seasonality, and repeatable logistics (transport, accommodation, and on-the-ground activities).

  • Wildlife: national parks and reserves with reliable viewing.
  • Culture: historic cities, UNESCO sites, markets, and local food.
  • Coast & islands: beaches, snorkeling/diving, and water sports.
  • Nature: mountains, deserts, and standout landscapes.
