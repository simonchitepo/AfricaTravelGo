FAQ

Is Africa suitable for kids?

Yes in many destinations with the right pacing, health preparation, and accommodation choices.

What’s the best itinerary style?

Shorter travel days, more rest days, and one base per region works well.

Do I need special health prep?

Consult a travel clinic for vaccines and malaria guidance for your route.

What’s the top mistake?

Stacking long transfer days that overwhelm children and parents.
