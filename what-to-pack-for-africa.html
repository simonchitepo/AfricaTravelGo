  • Pack to the strictest flight baggage rules.
  • Bring layers for early mornings and AC.
  • Prioritize sun protection and comfortable walking shoes.

Climate-based packing

Match your kit to your route:

  • Desert/highlands: big day-night temperature swings.
  • Coastal: humidity, light rain layers.
  • Safari: early drives are cold; dust is common.

FAQ

Should I pack different gear for safari vs cities?

Yes—safari mornings can be cold, and neutral colors plus dust-ready items help.

Do I need special clothing for religious sites?

In many places, modest coverage is respectful—carry a light layer or scarf.

How do I avoid overpacking?

Use a capsule wardrobe and plan laundry; pack to the strictest baggage leg.

What’s the most forgotten item?

A reliable power bank and plug adapter.
