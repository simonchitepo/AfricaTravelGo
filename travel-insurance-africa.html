  • Medical + evacuation coverage is the core.
  • Confirm coverage for activities (safari, hiking).
  • Know what documentation you need for claims.

FAQ

Do I need travel insurance?

It’s strongly recommended—especially for medical and evacuation coverage.

What coverage matters most?

Emergency medical and evacuation are usually the top priorities.

Does insurance cover safaris?

Often yes, but confirm your activities and regions are covered.

What’s the biggest mistake?

Buying the cheapest plan without checking exclusions and documentation requirements.
