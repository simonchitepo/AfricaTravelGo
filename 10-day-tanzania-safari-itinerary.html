10-Day Tanzania Safari Itinerary (Serengeti + Ngorongoro)

A high-value circuit that prioritizes time in the Serengeti and the Ngorongoro Crater with reasonable transfer days.

At a glance

DayBasePlan
Day 1ArushaArrive, gear check, and briefing. Early night.
Day 2Tarangire / ArushaDay safari in Tarangire or relax; adjust based on season.
Day 3SerengetiTransfer to Serengeti; afternoon game drive.
Day 4SerengetiFull day: follow wildlife concentrations by season; optional sunrise drive.
Day 5SerengetiSecond full day: different sector; track migration locations if relevant.
Day 6SerengetiThird full day: deep safari time (best sightings happen with time).
Day 7Ngorongoro Conservation AreaTransfer toward Ngorongoro; scenic stops.
Day 8Ngorongoro CraterCrater descent and game drive; return to rim by afternoon.
Day 9ArushaReturn to Arusha; optional cultural visit or rest.
Day 10DepartureFly out or continue to Zanzibar.

Pacing and logistics

  • Start early for wildlife drives and long transfers.
  • Keep 1 buffer slot for weather or delays.
  • Confirm domestic flight baggage limits if flying between parks.

FAQ

FAQ

How many days do I need for 10-Day Tanzania Safari Itinerary (Serengeti + Ngorongoro)?

Most travelers get a strong first visit in 2–4 days. Add extra time if you want day trips, slower pacing, or a beach/safari extension.

What’s the best time to visit 10-Day Tanzania Safari Itinerary (Serengeti + Ngorongoro)?

Aim for the main dry/shoulder season to balance weather and prices. Check the page’s seasonality notes for month-by-month guidance.

Is 10-Day Tanzania Safari Itinerary (Serengeti + Ngorongoro) safe for tourists?

Safety is manageable with standard precautions: use registered transport at night, keep valuables low-key, and follow local guidance on neighborhoods and scams.
