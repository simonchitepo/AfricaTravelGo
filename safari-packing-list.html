  • Soft-sided luggage for small aircraft.
  • Neutral layers for mornings and evenings.

The essentials

A simple checklist:

  • Neutral layers + warm top for mornings
  • Wide-brim hat + sunscreen
  • Insect repellent
  • Binoculars + small daypack
  • Power bank + charging cables

FAQ

Do I need camouflage clothing?

No—avoid camouflage in many countries. Neutral earth tones are best.

How cold are morning game drives?

Often chilly. Bring a warm layer and wind protection.

Should I bring binoculars?

Yes—one of the best-value items for wildlife viewing.

Can I do laundry on safari?

Many lodges offer laundry; confirm ahead, especially in remote camps.
