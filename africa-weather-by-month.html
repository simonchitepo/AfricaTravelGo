FAQ

Is rainy season a bad time to visit?

Not necessarily. It can mean greener landscapes, fewer crowds, and lower prices—plus short intense downpours.

Does weather affect safaris?

Yes—rain can affect road access and wildlife movement; shoulder seasons can be excellent.

Which region is best year-round?

It depends on your goals. Coastal and equatorial climates behave differently from southern regions.

What’s the top weather mistake?

Packing for one climate when your itinerary includes multiple regions and elevations.
