FAQ

Which cities work best for remote work?

Major hubs with stable infrastructure tend to be easiest. Validate connectivity with recent reviews.

How do I handle power outages?

Carry a power bank, consider a small UPS, and choose accommodation with a generator.

Is coworking available?

In many major cities yes; options vary by country.

What’s the best strategy?

Start with one ‘easy’ hub city, then add more adventurous legs after you’re settled.
